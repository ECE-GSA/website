About ECE-GSA@UCR

What is ECE-GSA?

Liaison between the graduate students and the department of ECE and CEN.

Who are members of ECE-GSA?

All ECE and CEN graduate students with good standing are members by default. To become an active member, register with the ECE-GSA via HighlanderLink and Slack
